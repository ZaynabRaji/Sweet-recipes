Classic chewy brownie recipe

This is a recipe to make some delicious brownies

ingredients :

  • 3 eggs
  • 335g white sugar
  • 115g plain flour
  • 30g Dutch cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions :

  1. Preheat oven to 180C
  2. Place butter and chocolate in a heatproof bowl over a saucepan of simmering water (don't let the bowl touch the water).
    Stir with a metal spoon until melted
  3. Quickly stir in egg, sugar, flour, cocoa powder, vanilla and salt until just combined.
  4. Pour into prepared pan.
    Bake for 30 minutes or until a skewer inserted in the centre comes out with moist crumbs clinging.
    Set aside to cool completely.
